Step 2 — Exposure Ladder Logic

Banks approve higher limits when they see existing limits.

Example pattern:

$2k approval → easier $5k approval
$5k approval → easier $10k approval
$10k approval → easier $20k approval

This is called credit exposure signaling.

Your engine should simulate this.
