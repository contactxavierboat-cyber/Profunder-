The Missing Metric: Credit Velocity

Banks care less about how many inquiries exist and more about how fast activity is happening.

This is called velocity risk.

High velocity = possible credit seeking behavior → higher default probability.

⸻

What Your Engine Should Show

Add a section under Capital Readiness:

Credit Velocity

Inquiry Velocity: High

7 inquiries in last 60 days

Institutional tolerance: ≤ 2 inquiries / 90 days

Status: Velocity Suppression Active

Why This Is Powerful
